---
title: "PestMacro"
description: "Automatically kills pests in your Garden."
---

<Accordion title="General" icon="gear">
  - **Pest Macro Keybind**: Toggles the pest macro.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Drawings" icon="pen-swirl">
  - **Pest ESP**: Renders a box around pests.
  - **Pest Tracer**: Draws a line to pests.
  - **Outline Infested Plots**: Draws an outline around garden plots that have pests.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Misc" icon="sparkles">
  - **TP to Closest Infested Plot**: Keybind to teleport to the nearest plot with pests.
  - **Wardrobe Slot to use with macro**: Wardrobe slot to switch to for killing pests. Set to 0 to disable.
  - **Pet name to switch to during macro**: Pet to switch to for killing pests. Leave empty to disable.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Plots" icon="grid-2">
  - **Plots list**: A grid to blacklist or whitelist specific garden plots for the macro.
</Accordion>
