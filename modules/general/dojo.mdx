<Accordion title="Force " icon="boxing-glove">
  - **Min CPS**: min CPS amount for the force challenge
  - **Max CPS**: max CPS amount for the force challenge
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Discipline" icon="sword">
  - **Auto Combat**: automatically attacks zombies in the discipline challenge
  - **Rotation Speed**: rotations speed for Auto Combat
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Mastery" icon="bow-arrow">
  - **Target Selection**: ⤵

    <Expandable title="Modes">
      - **Only Red**
      - **Red + Yellow**
      - **All**
    </Expandable>
  - **Shoot In Advance Time**: time in MS, change it depending on your ping/settings
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Swiftness" icon="walker">
  - **Suspicious Movement**: manipulates motion

    <Danger>
      This feature isn't bannable <u>for now</u>, but it might be in the future
    </Danger>
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Control" icon="person-running">
  - **Advance Time**: time in MS, increase if you have high ping
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Move Inventory" icon="sparkles">
  - **Move All Items**: if empty inventory, it moves the items 1 to 1 from container. Otherwise it moves all the items 1 to 1 to inventory
</Accordion>
