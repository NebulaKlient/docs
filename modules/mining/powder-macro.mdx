<Accordion title="Settings" icon="gear" iconType="duotone">
  - **Main Macro**: Keybind to start and stop the powder mining macro.
  - **Rotation Speed**: Controls how fast rotations move while mining and navigating.
  - **Max Explorer Perk**: Uses max exploror perk to open chests when enabled.
  - **Stay In Current Region**: Keeps the macro within the current Crystal Hollows region instead of exploring other areas.
  - **Amplitude Yaw**: Controls the horizontal rotation amplitude.
  - **Amplitude Pitch**: Controls the vertical rotation amplitude.
  - **Rotation Time**: Duration for rotations in milliseconds.
  - **Rotation Eccentricity**: Controls the randomness/variation in rotations for more natural movement.
</Accordion>
