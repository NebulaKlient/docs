---
title: "🛡️ FailsafeReactions"
description: "Human-like reactions for macro checks"
---

There's not much to configure here, as the module is designed to work in the background as a dependency to provide human-like reactions during macro checks. However, you can adjust the following setting:

<Accordion title="General" icon="gear">
  - **Send Chat Messages**: Occasionally sends chat messages to the server while a reaction is playing.
</Accordion>
