<Accordion title="Auto Sell" icon="sparkles">
  - **Auto sell during macro execution:** Will automatically pause current macro when your inventory is full and sell your inventory to either bazaar or npc depending on item.
  - **Auto sell runes:** Will also sell runes to npc's.
    - **Keep legendary runes**: Won't sell any runes of legendary tier.
  - **Auto sell custom items via trade menu**: Will sell any items you put in the custom input.
    - **Example usage**: "Item1, Item2" - separate items with commas.
