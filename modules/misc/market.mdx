<Accordion title="Auto Sell" icon="sparkles">
  - **Auto sell during macro execution:** Automatically pauses the current macro when your inventory is full and sells your inventory to either the bazaar or NPC depending on the item.
  - **Auto sell runes:** Includes runes in the auto sell process.
    - **Keep legendary runes**: Excludes legendary runes from being sold.
  - **Auto sell custom items via trade menu**: Checks for custom items to sell by their name.
    - **Example usage**: "Item1, Item2" - separate items with commas.
</Accordion>
