<Accordion title="   General" description="" icon="gears" iconType="duotone">
  - **Auto Come Back:** Jumps back into the rift after buying **Dimensional Infusion** from the wizard.
  - **Payment Mode:** Choice to pay with either **Bits or XP Bottles** when joining back.
  - **Show Macro HUDs in their respective locations:** Displays the macro HUD even if the macro is not enabled.
  - **Rotation Time Multiplier:** The rotation speed multiplier controls how fast rotations move - higher values make them slower. Best practice is to make the rotations look like something you can mimic.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Farming" icon="farm" iconType="solid">
  - **Agaricus Cap Auto Clicker:** Clicks for you when the timing is right.
    <Tip>
      This is just a helper when doing this manually. This doesn't have to be enabled for the **macro** itself as it does this automatically.
    </Tip>
  - **Agaricus Cap Macro:** Runs around **Dread Farm** and farms them for you.
  - **Wilted Berberis Macro:** Keybind to start the macro. Starts farming inside the field you press your bind in.
  - **Wilted Berberis Turbo Mode:** Experimental mode for faster farming.
  - **Disable Turbo Mode Player Range:** Disables turbo mode if a player is within X blocks. Set to 0 to disable this check.
  - **Rotation Time Multiplier**: Adjusts rotation speed specifically for the Wilted Berberis Macro.
  - **Delay After Berberis Break:** Delays your rotation to the next crop by X delay in ms set on the slider.
  - **Farm land priority:**
  - **Nearest**: Locates and starts the macro on the closest available farmland.
  - **Smallest**: Locates and starts the macro on the smallest available farmland.
  - **Enabled in**: Initiates farming in the field where the macro was started.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Mining" description="" icon="pickaxe" iconType="duotone">
  - **Timite Macro**: Keybind to start the macro.
  - **Increase Hitbox of ores**: Makes ores easier to click.
  - **Timite Macro Ore Type:**
  - **Youngite**: Targets Youngite.
  - **Timite**: Uses a Time Gun and targets Timite.
  - **Obsolite**: Uses a Time Gun and targets Obsolite.
  - **Highlite (Auto Craft)**: Auto crafts your choice while mining.
  - **Discrite (Auto Craft)**: Auto crafts your choice while mining.
  - **Timite Macro Rotation Speed**: The rotation speed for the Timite macro.
  - **Auto Stun Snakes**: Automatically mines and swaps between your **Frozen Water Pungi** and **Self-Recursive Pickaxe**.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Other" description="" icon="option" iconType="duotone">
  - **Sun Gecko Macro**: Automates fighting Sun Gecko.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Crux" icon="ankh" iconType="duotone">
  - **ESP Scribe Blocks**: Highlights scribe blocks for better visibility.
  - **Auto Look At Scribe Blocks**: Automatically looks at all scribe blocks.
  - **Auto Destroy Frozille's Ice**: Automatically destroys all ice blocks before they explode.
  - **Crux Esp**: Highlights all crux's for better visibility.
  - **Splatter Heart Esp**: Highlights splatter hearts on the ground for better visibility.
  - **Splatter Heart Trace**: Shows a tracer to the hearts.
  - **Crux Macro**: Keybind to start the macro for killing crux mobs.
  - **Crux Macro Type**:
  - **All**: Targets any crux mob in vicinity.
  - **Shy**: Targets only shy's
  - **Shadow**: Targets only shadow's
  - **Volt**: Targets only volt's.
  - **Scribe**: Targets only scribe's.
  - **Frozille**: Targets only frozille's.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Mirrorverse" icon="reflect-vertical" iconType="duotone">
  - **Dance**: Automatically completes Tiny Dancer puzzle.
  - **Mirrorverse Second Punch Delay**: Delay for the first punch in the Dance puzzle. Adjust if you miss.
  - **Mirrorverse Second Punch Delay**: Delay for the second punch in the Dance puzzle. Adjust if you miss.
  - **Lava Maze Helper**: Shows which blocks are walkable.
  - **UpsideDown Parkour**: Shows which platforms are correct.
  - **Turbulator Parkour**: Shows all the blocks without using the Emmet Pointer.
  - **Crafting Room Mob ESP**: Shows the invisible mobs on your side.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Misc" icon="rectangle-history-circle-plus" iconType="duotone">
  - **Hacking Solver**: Automatically clicks and solves the hacking in Dread Farm.
  - **Auto Use Tuba**: Uses Tuba when off cooldown.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Vampire" icon="coffin-cross" iconType="duotone">
  - **Vampire Macro**: Keybind to start the vampire slayer macro.
  - **Auto Heal**: Uses the Healing Melon when your percentage is met automatically.
  - **Auto Heal Percentage**: Percentage slider to trigger healing at X hp.
  - **Auto Steak**: Swaps to Steak when you can kill with one hit to a vampire or mobs.
  - **Auto Ice**: Automatically uses the Holy Ice to tank damage like Mania and Twinclaws.
  - **Auto Quick Time Event**: Completes the Impel tasks automatically.
  - **Boss Aim Helper**: Aim Assist for your Vampire Slayer Boss
  - **Turn On Third Person In Mania**: When the mania phase begins, turns on Third Person.
  - **Auto Break Spring**: Automatically runs to the Killer Spring and breaks it.
  - **Auto Break Spring distance**: The distance from which to start breaking the spring.
  - **Auto Escape In Mania**: Runs to the green areas during Mania.
  - **Boss Mode**: Toggle this to have the macro automatically handle boss quests.
  - **Auto Slayer**: Enable this if you have Hypixel's Auto Slayer turned on.
  - **Ignore Vampires That Has Players Nearby**: Prioritizes Thralls and Fledglings further away from other players.
  - **Vampire Macro Items Mode**:
    - **Sell**: When inventory is full, walks to the Motes Grubber and sells sellable items.
    - **Store in Ender Chest**: When inventory is full, stores your loot in your ender chest.
</Accordion>
