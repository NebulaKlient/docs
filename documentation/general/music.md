<summary>Playback Controls</summary>



* **Play/Pause:** Pauses or Resumes current song depending on previous state.\


- **Next Song:** Skips to the next song.



* **Previous Song:** Goes back to the previous song.



* **Mute Volume**: Mutes your spotify volume.



* **Decrease Volume**: Decreases your spotify volume once per press.



* **Increase Volume**: Increases your spotify volume once per press.
